The culture & Team

The culture and values

Accessibility is a fundamental right for people with disabilities, and a moral duty for creators. This is why we have decided to create a hub where studios can meet accessibility experts as well as players with disabilities. By doing so we take game productions to high standards of inclusion.

At Games Accessibility Hub, these are the values that define us:

  • Listening
  • Integrity
  • Collaboration
