We help you make more accessible games.

Why should you care?

An estimated 2.9 billion people — more than one out of every three people on the planet — played a video game in 2021.

According to the World health organization, an estimated 1.3 billion people experience significant disabilities. This represents 16% of the World’s population, or 1 in 6 of us.

People with disabilities want to play video games, too. And they can - as long as these games are accessible to them. They represent an underserved market of 400 to 500 million gamers, eager to play, like anyone else.

At Games Accessibility Hub, we want to give players with disabilities the opportunity to play and enjoy more accessible video games. To this end, we assist game creators, studios, and publishers, in better understanding the needs of gamers with disabilities, and fulfilling them. We help them set the lore, skill tree and win conditions that will make them create more accessible games.

How we can help

We provide a range of services tailored to your needs and context. Your project is unique, so is our support, based on those pillars:

  • Workshops
