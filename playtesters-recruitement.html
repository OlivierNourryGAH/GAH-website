Your experience matters

When it comes to check whether a game is accessible or not, there is no match for players with disabilities.

As a hub, we are always looking for accessibility experts and players with disabilities to test games during production. These playtests are of course compensated.

Contact form

Please fill and submit this form with your details. Ensure you have read and consented to our data policy. Applicants must be 16 years old at least.

This is not a registration form. It is meant to help us know you better and see if there is ground for a future collaboration. Submitting your details here does not guarantee that you will be able to participate in one of our operations.

We will study every application. If you do not hear from us within 20 business days, your application probably didn't retain our attention. It is not necessary to candidate multiple times, unless your information has changed significantly, or if you wish to modify your contact details.

If we do contact you, however, we may propose a remote meeting with you, in order to validate your application, and see how we can work together.
